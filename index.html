Dirt and Dogs

About Me

I'm a career mom that has spent half of my life in television operations. During Covid I really started struggling with my mental health and have found that dirt and dogs has been a guiding light to keep me on a positive track. The "dirt" is working in my yard by either landscaping or gardening. Growing things and watching them develop and thrive is rewarding and can give you a purpose. The dogs are of course my dogs and the dogs that I have fostered.
